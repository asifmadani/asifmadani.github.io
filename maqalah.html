📖 Maqalah

Lessons grounded in the Quran and authentic Sunnah — for students at every level.

Topics of Study

Aqeedah — Islamic Creed

Understanding the fundamentals of belief: Tawheed, the attributes of Allah, and the pillars of Iman as taught by Ahlus-Sunnah wal-Jama'ah.

Tafsir — Quranic Exegesis

In-depth explanations of Quranic verses, their contexts, linguistic nuances, and practical lessons for daily life.

Hadith Sciences

Study of the Prophet's ﷺ traditions — their chains of narration, authenticity, and application in contemporary contexts.

Fiqh — Islamic Jurisprudence

Rulings on worship, transactions, family law, and everyday matters drawn from the four major schools of thought.

Seerah — Prophetic Biography

The life of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ — his character, trials, teachings, and timeless lessons for the modern Muslim.

Tasawwuf & Tazkiyah

Spiritual purification of the heart — combating spiritual diseases and nurturing love of Allah and His Messenger ﷺ.

Islamic History

The rise of the Islamic civilisation, the Caliphates, great scholars of the past, and lessons from Muslim history.

Contemporary Issues

Islamic perspective on modern-day challenges: bioethics, finance, interfaith dialogue, and the role of Muslims in today's world.
